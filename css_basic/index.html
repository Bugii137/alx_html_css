Visibility and Personal Branding

Why Visibility and Personal Branding Matter

Key Idea: If recruiters can’t find you, they can’t hire you.

In today’s competitive tech landscape, coding skills alone aren’t enough to land opportunities. Employers, collaborators, and mentors often look for a digital footprint that showcases your work, personality, and passion.

Your online presence is often your first impression.

Showcasing your work publicly builds credibility.

Visibility brings unexpected opportunities: referrals, freelance gigs, and learning networks.

That’s where building a personal brand and being visible online come in.
